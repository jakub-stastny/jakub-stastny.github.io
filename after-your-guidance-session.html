After your session

First of all, thank you for allowing me to guide you. It's always an honour and responsibility that I never take lightly.

Now let's look at some things you can do for yourself to really take the best advantage of your session and to strengthen your commitment to your spiritual, mental and physical health:

  • If you end up emotionally destabilised, which might happen after touching deep issues that hasn't been attented for a long time, don't hesitate to contact me. Remember that I'm here for you.
  • Repeated sessions are strongly recommended as any real change require commitment and sustained effort. They are also cheaper!
  • I'm always looking for feedback! That is, I'm interested in honest feedback in order to know what could I improve. Pat on the shoulder feels nice, but that's not the point here.

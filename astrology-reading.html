How can astrology be useful

Astrology helps you to understand yourself better. Any question that helps you do that is a good question.

On the contrary questions that just want to know the future are pointless. "I just need to know" is an obsessive thought and leads you nowhere, but to waste your time.

Not to mention that the future is not fixed. We're in a process of co-creation. Parts of the energies are given, these are the ones astrology can tell you about. But what you do with these is up to you.

These "empty" questions include when and who do you marry. You should instead try to understand what kind of partner complements you best and what are your subconscious issues that prevent you from having a healthy relationship.

Another one is "will I get a job?". You shouldn't be concerned with a job, you should be trying to figure out what is it that you really want to spend your life on and how to get there.

So even though we can't do just about anything and free will absolutely does have its limitations, still, fatalistic approach is unhelpful. The question isn't "what awaits me", but rather how can I work with these energies that are already showing in my chart in order to become who I'm meant to be.

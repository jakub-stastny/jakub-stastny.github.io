About me

I'm dedicated to accompany and guide people in their healing journey.

That is the biggest passion of my life, something far greater than either career, money or a label I can hang on the illusionary self.

Some might think it's too good to be true. Obviously I could do other things in order to make more money, but I'm just not interested. I used to earn a lot of money while being disconnected, lonely and depressed.

So I don't use money or social status as a criterion of success. I don't think about success at all actually. My motivation simply is inner fulfillment and a feeling of aliveness and Truth.

And from this place of inner tranquility I feel compelled to help to spread peace, love and wisdom so others can live it too.

Ideals are great, but what do you eat? Or do you just have rich parents?

I know from my own experience that when one follows his own path in life, he'll be given enough. I don't have rich parents and I'm not rich myself, but I always get what I need on my path, so there's no reason for me to worry.

That's not to say I never work for money. Apart from accepting donations for energy healings I offer locally, I do various things that come my way such as cooking & selling food or coding.

One way or the other I'm no longer forced to sit in a cubicle and then having to drink in the evening in order to make myself forget that I'm wasting my life away. I love to cook and to code, so there's no inner contradiction. And I only say yes to work if I really mean it.

So then who are you really?

Who am I as a person doesn't matter. I had a conventional career, I had money and I travelled extensively. I'm neither a 24 year old hippie untouched by real life nor a middle-aged depressed guy who lives in his mum's basement.

I had a lot of struggles in my life until something within me changed and now I feel in peace, connected to all Life and there's nearly always a gentle underlaying joy as a background to my daily state of being, no matter what might be happening at the surface.

That is essentially what I want to pass on: that the grief can end. That the pain can go away. No matter the life situation.

And If you really want to know about me personally, feel free to ask me. I'm quite social when it comes to it.

How can you help?

I had people commenting on my clarity and ability to see the bigger picture for as long as I remember.

So when I started my journey towards a life of insight, everything finally clicked together. I realised this is something I not only like to do, but also something I'm actually really good at.

I was fascinated by psychology and philosophy from a very young age. Over the years I learnt a lot about various subjects, particularly buddhism, taoism, Jung, therapy, psychology and astrology.

I've been offering energy healings locally and learnt a lot through that. I'm very sensitive to energy and I often receive guidance from the spiritual realm.

Again none of these really matter. My own mentor knows quite little about various philosophies for instance, yet she's the most remarkable being I've ever known.

It does make people comfortable though, hence the list. Really the only credential that's required is I am. That might not make any sense in the beginning, but eventually it makes all the sense in the world.

I believe the future of humanity is not lost and can be very bright, but we're at a critical stage when we must transcend our mind – or cease to exist as a species.

My life is dedicated to help in this transition.

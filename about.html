About me

I'm dedicated to accompany and guide people in their healing journey.

That is the biggest passion of my life, something far greater than either career, money or a label I can hang on the illusionary self.

Some might think it's too good to be true. Obviously I could do other things in order to make more money, but I'm just not interested. I used to earn a lot of money while being disconnected, lonely and depressed.

So I don't use money or social status as a criterion of success. I don't think about success at all actually. My motivation simply is inner fulfillment and a feeling of aliveness and Truth.

Ideals are great, but what do you eat? Or do you just have rich parents?

I know from my own experience that when one follows his own path in life, he'll be given enough. I don't have rich parents and I'm not rich myself, but I always get what I need on my path, so there's no reason for me to worry.

That's not to say I never work for money. In my real life, I do energy healings and I accept donations for the work. When needed, I do various things that come my way such as cooking & selling food or coding.

One way or the other I'm no longer forced to sit in a cubicle and then having to drink in the evening in order to make myself forget that I'm wasting my life away. I love to cook and to code, so there's no inner contradiction.

So then who are you really?

Who I am as a person doesn't matter. I had a conventional career, I had money and I travelled extensively. So I'm neither a 24 yo hippie untouched by real life nor a middle-aged depressed guy without a job living in his mum's basement.

I had a lot of struggles in my life until something within me changed and now I feel in peace, connected to all Life and there's nearly always a gentle underlaying joy as a background to my daily state of being.

That is essentially what I want to pass on: that the grief can end. That the pain can go away. No matter the life situation.

If you really want to know about me personally, feel free to ask me. I'm quite social when it comes to it.

How can you help?

I had people commenting on my clarity and ability to see the bigger picture for as long as I remember. So when I started with my journey towards a life of insight, everything clicked together.
